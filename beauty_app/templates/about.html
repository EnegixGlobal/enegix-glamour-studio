Who We Are

A Passion for Beauty,
Born in Mumbai

Glamour Studio was founded in 2014 with a singular vision — to create a space where luxury, artistry, and personalised care come together to offer an experience unlike any other in the city.

From our humble beginnings as a boutique studio in Bandra West, we have grown into one of Mumbai's most celebrated beauty destinations — trusted by celebrities, brides, and thousands of loyal clients across the city.

Our philosophy is simple: every person deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and celebrated. Every service we offer is a reflection of that belief.
